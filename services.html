Our Services

Research Offering

The history team has extensive collective experience in conducting research and can support various requirements and various topics. Our services include conducting subject research to identify key concepts, determining timetables, finding quotations, fact checking, and identifying complementary collections. We also provide curators and planners with a wide range and thorough display items for exhibition and interpretation procedures.

Interpretive Writing

Knowing History provides explanatory writing services for exhibition texts, virtual exhibitions, commemorative plaques, self-guided tours, promotional materials and multimedia presentations. Our employees know how to create attractive, accessible and inclusive text. After consulting the museum staff to understand the organization's target audience, our employees will use words and concepts familiar to museum audiences to carefully craft text suitable for appropriate reading levels.

Cataloguing and Digitization

Know History colleagues are trained and experienced in classifying cultural relics, documents, photos and artworks. We provide a range of services, from simple classification and organization to the development of classification guidelines and technical solution recommendations. We also provide various digital services to support our clients' goals. Our employees have received training in records management, fragile material handling, photo digitization, and audio/video conversion.
